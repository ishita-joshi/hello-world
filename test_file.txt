The Race

Once upon a time, a speedy rabbit boasted to all the animals that no one could beat him in a race. A quiet turtle stepped forward and accepted the challenge.

On race day, the rabbit dashed ahead and, confident of his lead, stopped to nap under a shady tree. The turtle plodded on — one steady step at a time.

When the rabbit awoke, he sprinted to the finish line, only to find the turtle already there, smiling.

“Slow and steady wins the race,” said the turtle.
The rabbit never bragged again.
